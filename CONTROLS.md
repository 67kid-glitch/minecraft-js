# CONTROLS

## Player 1 (Controller)
Left Stick  - Move  
Right Stick - Camera  
A           - Jump  
B           - Character Action  
X           - Interact / Talk  
Y           - Special Ability  
L / R       - Switch Character  
+           - Pause  

## Player 2 (Optional Co-op, Keyboard)
WASD        - Move  
Mouse       - Camera  
Space       - Jump  

Controls are action-based, not button-based.
Each character interprets actions differently.
